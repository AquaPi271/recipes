Ingredients:

1.5 lbs chicken breast
1.0 lbs top sirloin steak
1 green pepper
1 red pepper
1 sweet onion
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1 teaspoon garlic
pinch of ginger

1)  Chop vegetables into slices, approximately 1 inch square.
2)  Like-wise chop meat into 1 inch squares.
3)  Mix honey, teriyaki sauce, garlic and ginger in a large freezer bag.
4)  Place all ingredients in the bag.
5)  Let sit for 4-24 hours.
6)  Empty bag of marinade.
7)  On skewers place ingredients.
8)  Cook on 300 degree heat on the grill for 10 minutes, rotating 1/4 turn every
    2.5 minutes.
9)  Remove from skewers and serve.
